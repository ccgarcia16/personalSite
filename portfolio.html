The Two Fridas

Completed shortly after her divorce with Diego Rivera, this portrait displays Frida's duel personalities. One is traditional, heartbroken, the other is independent and dressed modern. In her diary she said it was based from a memory of an imaginary friend from childhood. Later she admitted it expressed loneliness from her divorce.
